About Me

I'm the co-founder and CTO of LearnOBots, an EdTech company rethinking education through playful and fun learning.

I'm a technologist, product designer, entrepreneur, a maker and a former teacher. My work involves designing products in the EdTech domain — some of which include SaaS based platforms, robots for education, STEAM based hands-on curriculums and content for classrooms.

I'm always interested in using Tech for good and solving problems in developing countries. I also love projects that intersect the domains of Design, Technology and Art.
