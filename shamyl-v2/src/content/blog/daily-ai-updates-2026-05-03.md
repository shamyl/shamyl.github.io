## AI Developments

### Meta Enters Humanoid Robotics
- [**Meta acquires Assured Robot Intelligence (ARI)**](https://about.meta.com/), a humanoid robotics startup, signaling a major push into physical AI. This acquisition brings Meta into direct competition with [Tesla's Optimus](https://www.tesla.com/optimus) and other robotics players. Meta plans to build AI models for humanoid robots.

### Pentagon's Classified AI Deals
- [The Pentagon](https://www.defense.gov/) has struck **classified AI deals with 7 major tech companies** including [OpenAI](https://openai.com/), [Google](https://www.google.com/), and [Nvidia](https://www.nvidia.com/). Notably, [**Anthropic was excluded**](https://www.anthropic.com/) from these contracts, raising questions about the competitive landscape in defense AI.

### OpenAI's Strategic Focus
- [**Sam Altman**](https://twitter.com/sama) outlined [OpenAI's three key focus areas](https://openai.com/) for its next phase of growth. The company continues to build frontier models while expanding its enterprise partnerships, with GPT-5.5 now available through [Microsoft Foundry](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-foundry/).

### Google Confirms Gemini-Powered Siri
- [Google](https://www.google.com/) has **confirmed that [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) will power the next-generation [Siri](https://www.apple.com/siri/)** for Apple devices, expected to launch later this year. This multi-year deal marks a significant AI partnership between the two tech giants.

## Technology Trends

### Microsoft's AI Agent Push
- [**Microsoft 365 E7 and Agent 365 are now generally available**](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365), marking Microsoft's full embrace of agentic AI in the workplace. [Copilot's](https://copilot.microsoft.com/) agentic capabilities are now available across [Word](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/word), [Excel](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/excel), and [PowerPoint](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/powerpoint).
- [**Windows 365 for Agents**](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-365) is now in public preview, allowing organizations to run AI agents securely at scale.

### NVIDIA's Rubin Platform
- [NVIDIA's **Vera Rubin platform**](https://www.nvidia.com/) continues to roll out with seven new chips designed specifically for agentic AI. The platform delivers extreme performance for AI training and inference at scale.

### Anthropic's Corporate Governance Crisis
- [Anthropic's](https://www.anthropic.com/) most powerful AI model has **exposed critical gaps in corporate AI governance frameworks**, prompting calls for new governance structures across banking, healthcare, and retail sectors.

## Market Updates

### Major Acquisitions This Week
- [**Nebius acquires Eigen AI**](https://nebius.com/) for **$643 million** to bring [MIT](https://www.mit.edu/) inference talent in-house, strengthening its position in AI infrastructure.
- [**Cohere acquires Germany's Aleph Alpha**](https://cohere.com/) in a **$20 billion deal** to build sovereign AI alternatives for European markets.
- [**Scout AI raised $100 million Series A**](https://www.scout-ai.com/) — the largest defense-tech Series A in U.S. history — to build AI systems for unmanned warfare.

### Meta's Bond Offering
- [Meta](https://investor.atmeta.com/) **raised $25 billion in bonds** as its stock dropped 9.5% on news of a **$145 billion AI spending plan**. The company is betting big on AI infrastructure despite investor concerns.

## World Tech

### U.S. and Allies Publish AI Security Guidance
- [**Five Eyes nations**](https://www.gcsb.govt.nz/publications/guidance/guidance-on-securing-artificial-intelligence/) (U.S., Australia, Canada, New Zealand, UK) jointly published guidance urging organizations to treat autonomous AI agents as critical security concerns, providing frameworks for safe deployment.

### FCC Tightens Telecom Rules
- [The FCC](https://www.fcc.gov/) approved new regulations to crack down on robocalling and close loopholes for banned foreign services, tightening KYC rules for telecoms and bolstering supply chain security.

### EU Tech Regulations Update
- [EU](https://ec.europa.eu/) and UK competition rules have been updated around **technology licensing**, while [Google](https://www.google.com/) received guidance from EU regulators on helping AI rivals access its services to comply with [Digital Markets Act](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/ip_22_6423) requirements.
- The EU is also urging fast rollout of age-verification apps to protect minors online.
