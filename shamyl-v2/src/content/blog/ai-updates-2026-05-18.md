## AI Developments

**OpenAI Codex Goes Mobile**
- OpenAI has integrated Codex into the ChatGPT mobile app, allowing developers to monitor and manage AI coding agents directly from their phones
- This positions OpenAI as a serious competitor to Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude in the coding agent space
- The update builds groundwork for OpenAI's upcoming "super app" vision

**Anthropic Overtakes OpenAI in Enterprise**
- For the first time, Anthropic has more verified business customers than OpenAI, according to Ramp data
- This signals growing enterprise trust in Claude's reliability and safety features
- A significant milestone in the competitive AI landscape

**Google DeepMind's AI Pointer**
- DeepMind revealed "Magic Pointer" — an AI-enabled mouse pointer that understands context around the cursor
- Powered by Gemini, it captures visual and semantic context without needing to switch windows
- Could fundamentally change how humans interact with computers

**OpenAI's Daybreak Security Initiative**
- OpenAI launched Daybreak, a cybersecurity platform pairing GPT-5.5 variants with Codex Security
- Designed to help defenders find vulnerabilities before attackers exploit them
- A direct response to Anthropic's Mythos in the cyber defense space
