Here's your daily roundup of AI and tech news for May 8, 2026:

## AI Developments

- **Google and Meta Race to Build Personal AI Agents**: Both companies are rushing to develop personal AI agents to compete with Anthropic and OpenAI, who have pulled ahead in this space. The race is intensifying as consumer demand for AI assistants grows.

- **Anthropic x SpaceX Partnership**: Anthropic announced a major compute deal with SpaceX to substantially boost AI computing power for Claude. This partnership addresses the critical compute bottleneck facing AI companies.

- **Enterprise AI Joint Ventures**: Both Anthropic and OpenAI are launching joint ventures focused on enterprise AI services, signaling a shift toward B2B revenue streams and custom enterprise solutions.

- **Government AI Model Testing**: Microsoft, Google, and xAI have agreed to let the U.S. government test their AI models before public launch, reflecting growing regulatory collaboration and preemptive safety measures.

## Technology Trends

- **Agentic AI Infrastructure**: Multiple major players launched tools for the "agentic era":
  - AWS announced the Agent Toolkit for AWS to help AI coding agents build effectively
  - Amazon SageMaker AI now features an AI agent experience for model customization
  - Twilio unveiled a new conversation layer and infrastructure at SIGNAL 2026
  - IBM delivered a blueprint for the AI Operating Model with next-gen agent orchestration

- **AI Governance Solutions**: Collibra launched an AI Command Center to help enterprises scale agentic AI with real-time oversight, addressing the growing "agent sprawl" problem.

## Market Updates

- **Record Q1 AI Funding**: AI companies raised $226B in Q1'26, surpassing 2025's full-year total. OpenAI's $122B corporate minority round alone comprised 54% of the quarter's funding.

- **SAP's €1.16B Bet**: SAP announced plans to acquire German AI startup Prior Labs (18 months old) for approximately $1.16 billion, signaling big tech's appetite for AI talent over revenue.

- **Meta's Physical AI Push**: Meta acquired a robotics AI startup as part of its strategy to expand into physical AI and embodied intelligence.

- **Global VC Activity**: April 2026 saw 1,366 deals with $212 billion deployed across startups worldwide, marking one of the busiest months for venture capital in recent years.

## World Tech

- **China's Moonshot AI Raises $2B**: Chinese AI startup Moonshot AI raised $2 billion at a $20 billion valuation as global demand for open-source AI solutions continues to accelerate.

- **EU Softens AI Act**: EU lawmakers struck a provisional deal to postpone restrictions on high-risk AI systems by more than a year, reflecting industry pressure and the need for more practical implementation timelines.

- **SpaceX Terafab Project**: Elon Musk's plans for a Texas chip fab (Terafab) will cost up to $119 billion, with the first phase at $55 billion. The facility will produce chips for Tesla, SpaceX, and xAI.

## Key Takeaway

The theme for May 2026 is clear: **compute is the new oil, and agents are the new interface.** Anthropic's SpaceX partnership, SAP's billion-dollar acquisition of a 1.5-year-old startup, and the EU's AI Act delay all point to a market prioritizing AI infrastructure and deployment speed over regulatory caution. For CTOs, the agentic AI wave is here—organizations that build agent orchestration capabilities now will have a significant advantage.
