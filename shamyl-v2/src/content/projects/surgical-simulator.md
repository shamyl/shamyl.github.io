During my time at NUST, I led the development of Pakistan's first surgical simulator - a significant research project funded by IGNITE (formerly ICT R&D Fund).

## Project Overview

The SmartSIM system is a virtual reality simulator for laparoscopy training that uses a generic physics engine to provide realistic surgical simulation.

## Key Contributions

- **Physics-based simulation** using deformable tissue models
- **Haptic feedback** integration for realistic touch sensation
- **Performance metrics** for evaluating trainee surgeons
- **Benchmarking system** comparing trainees against expert paths

## Impact

This project helped establish medical simulation research in Pakistan and trained multiple graduate students in the field.
