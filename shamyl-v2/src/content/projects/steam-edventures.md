STEAM Edventures is a game-based learning platform that makes STEAM education accessible and fun.

## Game Concept

Players embark on adventures where they must use coding, electronics, and problem-solving skills to progress through levels and complete challenges.

## Learning Outcomes

- **Electronics fundamentals** - circuits, components, sensors
- **Programming basics** - loops, conditionals, functions
- **Problem-solving** - logical thinking and debugging
- **Creativity** - designing solutions to open-ended challenges

## Target Audience

Designed for students aged 8-14, with content that scales in complexity as players advance.
