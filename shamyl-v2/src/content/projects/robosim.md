RoboSim is an educational simulator designed to make learning programming fun and engaging for children.

## Concept

Instead of abstract coding concepts, students program virtual robots and characters to complete missions and solve puzzles in a 3D environment.

## Features

- **Block-based programming** for beginners
- **Text-based Python** for advanced learners
- **Multiple characters** and environments
- **Progressive difficulty** levels
- **Curriculum integration** with school standards

## Educational Approach

RoboSim follows a "learn by doing" philosophy where students see immediate visual feedback from their code, making debugging intuitive and learning enjoyable.
