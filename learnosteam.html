LearnOSTEAM

LearnOSTEAM is world's first most comprehensive platform for hands on STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts & Mathematics) education designed for schools.

Company: LearnOBots

This product was developed at LearnOBots.

My Role

The concept was co-created by me and my co-founder. I did the initial research, came up with the first design and architecture and eventually had it implemented with my in-house development team, which I hired and managed. This platform has now thousands of users and growing.

Details

LearnOSTEAM is a SaaS based platform for STEAM education that enables schools to implement the hands on STEAM approach in their schools. It consists of hands on lessons in many different domains. From 10 different coding languages, to electronics to robotics, AI, 3D modeling and many others interactive lessons that students go through. If you or any school is interested you can check it out here
